A full stack forum web application using typescript, react, node, express, postgreSQL. Users can register, login, logout, and change password when they forgot their passwords. Users are able to create a post and edit or delete it. Not only users can create a post, but also create comment on other posts or even upvote or downvote it. There are 11 categories to categorize all of the posts and the posts of the top category will be displayed.
